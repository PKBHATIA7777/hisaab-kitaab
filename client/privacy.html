Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Hisaab-Kitaab, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide our services:

  • Personal Information: Name and Email Address (via Google Login or Manual Signup).
  • Usage Data: Expense records, group names ("Chapters"), and settlement data you create.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the functionality of the app:

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To calculate expenses and display balances between friends.
  • To send important account notifications (e.g., password resets).

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure server connections (SSL), to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.
